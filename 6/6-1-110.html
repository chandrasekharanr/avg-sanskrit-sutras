वृत्ति: एङो ङसिँङसोरति पूर्वरूपमेकादेश: । In place of a preceding एङ् (“ए”, “ओ”) letter and the following short “अ” of the affix “ङसिँ” or “ङस्”, there is a single substitute of the former (एङ् letter.)
